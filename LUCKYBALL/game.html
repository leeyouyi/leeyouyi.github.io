Background of the Lucky Airship

As a representative of high speed aquatics, the highest speed of a racing motor boat can reach to 250km/hour, while the engine spinning 9600 times per minute, the motor boat could fly above the water surface; this is the reason why we named it the Lucky Airship.

From tailors to pilots, everyone has a dream of flying inside our heart, we all wanted to escape gravity and let us become freer. The arrival of the Lucky Airship can make everyone’s dream come true.
